About Indonesia

The Heart of Southeast Asia

Indonesia is the world's largest archipelago, consisting of more than 17,000 islands spread across Southeast Asia.

Home to over 270 million people, Indonesia is one of the most culturally diverse countries in the world.

Indonesia has more than 1,300 ethnic groups and over 700 local languages, making it rich in traditions, culture, and heritage.

Indonesia consists of five major islands:

  • Sumatra
  • Java
  • Kalimantan
  • Sulawesi
  • Papua
